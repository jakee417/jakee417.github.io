Wǒ jiào dài àolún。wǒ shì méiguó rén。wǒ jīn nián sān shí sān suì。wǒ zhù zài Issaquah，zài xīyǎtú pángbiān。wǒ de jiā zài túshūguǎn pángbiān。wǒ de gōngzuò shì chéngxùyuán。

<details><summary>Translation</summary>
My name is Jake Taylor. I am american. I am 33 years old. I live in Issaquah, near Seattle. My house is near the library. I am a programmer.
</details>

---

wǒ xǐhuan wán diànnǎo yóuxì。wǒ měi gè zhōumò dōu zài jiā wán diànnǎo。wǒ gēn wǒ de lǎopó yīqǐ wán diànnǎo。wǒ de dìdi bù gōngzuò de shíhou tā yě wán。wǒmen yòng diànnǎo wán Stardew Valley。

<details><summary>Translation</summary>
I like to play computer games. Every weekend, I like to play with the computer at my house. My wife and I like to play the computer together. My little brother plays when he is not working. We use the computer to play Stardew Valley. 
</details>

---

Wán Stardew Valley de shíhou yě chī hǎochī de fàn，zǒulù qù péngyou de jiā，tīng yīngyuè hái dǎ yāoguài。wǒmen yě wán qítā yóuxì， dànshì Stardew Valley zuì hǎowán。wǒ hé wǒ de lǎopó xǐhuan Stardew Valley yīnwèi wán de shíhou wǒmen fēicháng kāixīn。

<details><summary>Translation</summary>
While we play Stardew Valley, we eat delicious food, walk to friend's houses, and listen to music while beating monsters. We also like to play other games, but Stardew Valley is the most fun. My wife and I like Stardew Valley because we feel happy.
</details>
