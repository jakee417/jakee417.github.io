[concept] Schwarzchild metric
Describes the geometry of space time caused by the presence of a black hole

[eqn] Schwarzchild metric
$$
d\tau^2 = (1 - \frac{2MG}{r'}) dt'^2 - (\frac{1}{1 - \frac{2MG}{r'}}) dr'^2 - r'^2d\Omega^2
$$

[eqn] Schwarzchild radius
$$
R_S = 2MG
$$


[eqn] Schwarzchild metric (universal form)
$$
d\tau^2 = R_S^2[(1 - \frac{1}{r}) dt^2 - (\frac{1}{1 - \frac{1}{r}}) dr^2 - r^2 d\Omega^2]
$$

[concept] Curvature (review)
Curvature is analogous to tidal forces. Tidal forces are the effect of curvature.

[eqn] Curvature Tensor
$$
\mathcal R_{srn}^t = \partial_r\Gamma_{sn}^t - \partial_s \Gamma_{rn}^t
 + \Gamma_{sn}^p \Gamma_{pr}^t - \Gamma_{rn}^p \Gamma_{ps}^t
$$

[eqn] Christoffel symbol
$$
\Gamma_{mn}^t = \frac{1}{2}[\partial_n g_{rm} + \partial_m g_{rn} - \partial_r g_{mn}]
$$
